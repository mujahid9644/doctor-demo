Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our website using the appointment form, call us directly, or send us an email. We'll confirm your appointment within 24 hours.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most dental insurance plans. Please contact us to verify coverage for your specific plan.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant medical history. Arrive 15 minutes early to fill out our patient form.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide emergency dental services. Call us immediately if you have a dental emergency, and we'll fit you in as soon as possible.

How often should I visit?

We recommend visiting every 6 months for regular checkups and cleanings. However, the frequency may vary based on your individual oral health needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. Flexible payment plans are also available for larger treatments.
